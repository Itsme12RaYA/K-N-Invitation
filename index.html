Our Love Story

First Meet

March 08, 2017

Our story began when we studied at the same school, where we met as students. A friendship quickly blossomed as we shared notes, laughter, and support. Over time, our bond deepened, and we discovered common interests and dreams. What started as a simple friendship grew into a beautiful love. Now, we are excited to begin the next chapter of our lives as husband and wife, filled with joy and cherished memories.

First Date

March 25 , 2017

Our first date is a memory we both cherish deeply. It was an exciting evening, filled with the anticipation of new experiences and the flutter of burgeoning feelings. We decided to watch a movie together, and it turned out to be our first time experiencing a 3D cinema.But what made that evening truly special wasn't just the 3D effects or the plot of the film—it was the way we connected. Sharing popcorn, whispering jokes, and occasionally reaching for each other's hands, we found ourselves wrapped in a bubble of pure happiness. The magic of that night wasn't just on the screen; it was in the air between us.

In a Relationship

March 29 , 2017

From that day on, we were officially a couple—girlfriend and boyfriend. Every moment we spent together felt even more special, as we embraced our new roles and deepened our connection. Looking back now, we see that day as a pivotal point in our story, a day when friendship blossomed into love and set us on the path to where we are now, standing together, ready to say "I do."

Enagagement

February 14, 2024

It was Valentine’s Day, a day already filled with love and anticipation. It was perfect in its simplicity, a true reflection of us and our love. Tears filled my eyes as I said yes, and we embraced, knowing that this was just the beginning of a beautiful new chapter. Our engagement, though unexpected and unpretentious, was everything we could have hoped for – genuine, heartfelt, and absolutely perfect.
